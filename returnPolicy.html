    Return Policy

    At Kittens, we strive to match each of our beloved cats with the perfect forever home. We understand that sometimes circumstances change, and we want to ensure that both you and your new feline companion are happy. If for any reason you find that your new cat is not the right fit for your home, we're here to assist you.

    Returns

    We offer a 30 calendar-day return period for your new cat. During this time, you may return the cat to us for a refund or exchange if you find that it is not suitable for your household.

    Return Process

    To initiate a return, please contact our customer service team at Contact Us. Our dedicated team members will assist you with the return process, provide guidance, and address any concerns you may have.

    Upon contacting us, we will provide you with instructions on how to safely return the cat to us. We ask that you ensure the cat is returned to us in good health, with all necessary paperwork and accessories provided at the time of purchase.

    Refunds

    Once we receive the returned cat and verify its condition, we will proceed with processing your refund. Refunds will be issued to your original method of payment. Please allow some time for the refund to be processed and for the credit to reflect in your account, as processing times may vary depending on your financial institution.

    Exchanges

    If you would like to exchange your cat for another one within our available inventory, please contact our customer service team to arrange for an exchange. Exchanges are subject to availability and must be completed within the designated return period.

    Return Conditions

    Returned cats must be in good health and free from any injuries or illnesses not present at the time of purchase. Cats must also be returned with all necessary paperwork, including health records, registration documents, and any accessories provided at the time of purchase.

    Additional Considerations

    We understand that returning a pet can be a difficult decision, and we're here to support you every step of the way. Our team is available to answer any questions you may have and provide guidance to ensure the well-being of both you and your cat.

    Please note that while we strive to provide accurate information about each cat's temperament and behavior, individual personalities may vary. We recommend taking the time to get to know your new cat and allowing for an adjustment period to ensure a successful transition into your home.

    Contact Us

    If you have any questions or concerns about our return policy or need assistance with a return, please don't hesitate to contact our customer service team at Contact Us. We are committed to ensuring that you have a positive experience with your new cat from Kittens.
