    The Burmese cat is a medium-sized, muscular, and stocky domestic cat breed that originated in Burma, near the Thai-Burma border, and was developed in Britain and the United States. They have short coats that come in many colors, including sable, champagne, blue, platinum, lilac, fawn, red, cream, chocolate, cinnamon, and tortoiseshell.
